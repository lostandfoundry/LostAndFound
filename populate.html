Relationships

While thinking about this project, we narrowed down on 5 relationships, that effectively cover all possible human ties with other entities. Make sure to stick by them while creating your graph relations.

Delay

There might be a slight delay when it comes to adding nodes and relationships in the graph. Please be patient.

Rules and limitations

Maxmimum number of nodes per person is set to 15 as of yet. There is no upper bound on the number of relations, however it becomes imperative here to mention that multiple relations between 2 nodes would serve absolutely no purpose.
Nodes could be of the following types (case sensitive):-

  •   person
  •   pet
  •   other
Relationships could be of type (case sensitive):-
  •   friend
  •   relative
  •   neighbour
  •   colleague
  •   owner
  •   other
